Product Information

DermRange Moisturizer

Ingredients

Stearate (and) PEG-100 Stearate, Dimethicone, CCTG, Propylene Glycol, Glyceryl MonoStearate, Glycerin, Octyldodecanol, Phenoxyethanol, Ammonium Polyacryloyldimethyl Taurate, Allantoin, Tocopheryl Acetate, Polysorbate 80, Water & laminaria digitata extract & hydrogenated lecithin & cetyl-PG hydroxyethyl palmitamide & ceramide EOP & ceramide NS/ceramide NG & ceramide NP & ceramide AS & ceramide AP & cholesterol & 1,2-hexanediol, Fragrance

Directions for Use

  1. Apply a small amount evenly to cleansed face and neck.
  2. Use in the morning and evening for best results.
  3. Follow with sunscreen during the daytime.
  4. Store in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight.

Caution:

  • Avoid contact with eyes.
  • Discontinue use if irritation occurs.
  • Store in a cool, dry place. For external use only.

DermRange H2O Sunscreen

Ingredients

Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate, Titanium Dioxide, Zinc Oxide, Glycerin, Butyl Methoxydibenzoylmethane, Caprylic Acid, Bis-Ethylhexyloxyphenol Methoxyphenyl Triazine, Niacinamide, Butylene Glycol, Stearyl Stearate, Octocrylene, Hyaluronic Acid, Sunflower Seed Oil, Vitamin E, Allantoin, Centella Asiatica Extract, Physalis Extract, Sodium Benzoate, Vitamin C, Phenoxyethanol, Melanin.

Directions for Use

  1. Squeeze two lines on index and middle fingers.
  2. Apply liberally on face 15 minutes before sun exposure.
  3. Reapply every 2 hours, especially after swimming or sweating.

Caution:

  • Avoid contact with eyes.
  • Discontinue use if irritation occurs.
  • Store in a cool, dry place. For external use only.
